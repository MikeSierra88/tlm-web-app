Bio

Tyler Levs is an independent singer-songwriter and live music performer from Maine USA. While navigating soft folk, acoustic rock and catchy pop hooks Tyler has continued to grow as an artist across the internet and in the New England area.

Full-time as of 2019, he has streamed almost 9,000 hours on Twitch.TV, uploaded 300+ one take covers to Youtube, released original music in multiple genres on all streaming platforms, performed at the San Diego and Las Vegas Twitch Conventions, started a duo band with his songwriting partner Joliet4 (called MUTU4L) and continues to perfect his craft as a guitarist, loopstation artist and producer.

Tyler has performed at multiple bars and restaurants, private parties, as a supporting act for O-Town and at the New England music awards where he was also nominated and won Maine's Rising Artist.

To find out more please stop by a live Twitch Stream or contact him through various social platforms.
